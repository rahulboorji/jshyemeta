Our Company

J.S.HyE-META has been meticulously crafted by a team of dynamic, unwavering and passionate individuals who are dedicated to improving the world for future generations. It is headquartered in the Netherlands with extensive R&D and a pilot plant in the Indian subcontinent. We, as a budding enterprise, are focused on engineering innovative plastic-free biodegradable hybrid composite materials that redefine the boundaries of sustainability and circular economy in a diverse value chain. Our introductory product is an innovative, plastic-free, 100% biodegradable, circularly sustainable NFRC material with wide functional applicability. It has diverse applications in the transportation, architecture, packaging, and electronics sectors, with a huge market share of around 900 billion euros.
